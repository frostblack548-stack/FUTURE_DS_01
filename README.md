# 🛒 Superstore Sales Performance Dashboard

## 📊 Project Overview
This repository contains a comprehensive Business Intelligence dashboard designed to analyze and visualize the sales performance, profitability, and product trends of a retail superstore. Built using **Power BI**, this project transforms raw retail data into actionable insights, allowing stakeholders to easily track high-level KPIs and drill down into specific categories, regions, and timeframes.

## 🎯 Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)
The dashboard provides an immediate snapshot of overall business health through high-level metric cards:
* **Total Sales:** 2.30M
* **Total Profit:** 286.40K
* **Total Orders:** 5K
* **Total Quantity Sold:** 38K
* **Profit Margin:** 12% (0.12)

## 📈 Visualizations & Insights
The report is structured to provide both macro and micro views of the sales pipeline:

* **Time Series Analysis (Line Chart):** *Total Sales by Quarter and Month* tracks revenue velocity across the fiscal year, highlighting seasonal peaks and drops from Q1 through Q4.
* **Category Performance (Horizontal Bar Chart):** *Total Sales by Category* breaks down revenue generation across core business segments (Technology, Furniture, and Office Supplies).
* **Profitability Breakdown (Donut Chart):** *Total Profit by Category* highlights which segments are driving the bottom line. Notably, Technology accounts for over 50% of total profit, despite Furniture and Office Supplies having significant sales volume.
* **Granular Product View (Ranked Bar Chart):** *Total Sales by Product Name* identifies the top-performing individual SKUs and brands (e.g., Canon, Fellowes, Cisco).
* **Geographic Distribution (Column Chart):** *Total Sales by Region* compares performance across the West, East, Central, and South regions to identify top-performing markets.

## 🛠️ Tech Stack & Tools
* **Platform:** Microsoft Power BI
* **Languages:** DAX (Data Analysis Expressions) for calculated measures
* **Design/UI:** Custom thematic styling utilizing a clean, high-contrast blue and purple color palette for clear data readability.
